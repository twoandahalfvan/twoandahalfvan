How to prepare for vanlife

Judith February 13, 2020

From "What do you think of vanlife?" and "Would be nice to travel in a van!" to actually doing it, you come a long way. But how do you prepare for such a life changing event? Actually, you cannot - a least not to 100 %. 

Hello internet! 

First of all, you want to start searching YouTube, Instagram or Pinterest and also discussion boards online for vanlife stories. So many people are living in a van and luckily they document it online, all the pros and cons. You can learn a lot from other people's experiences, skills, successes and mistakes. Could this life be for you? 

Some examples of helpful content

  • https://forum.camper-bauen.de/ (awesome content, discussions, helpful vanlifers - only in German)

Do not only scavenge YouTube etc. for experiences though. Use it for inspiration and education as well. What solutions do you like? How did other vanlifers manage the questions you will come across: from electricity to carpentry. Start saving videos and ideas, pin them to boards or write them down. You will come back to them later while planning and building your van. For us, making up our mind about ideas and planning the van took even longer than actually building it. And we often asked ourselves: "Where have we seen the solution? What video was it again?"

Minimalistic thinking 

What do you really need in your life to be happy or thankful? Start thinking about your habits and definitely the materialistic objects that suround you. What makes you appreciate your time and makes you smile? What objects haven't you touched in years? Because here is the undeniable truth: Not every thing and not every hobby can go with you! This is a good thing though. We consume and buy far too much new stuff anyway. Vanlife is the big chance for you to realize: You really don't need much to feel good. Think about it: When moving to another place have you ever noticed how much stuff relocates untouched from box to another box? Or how much things are thrown away?

Well, moving into a van is just like that, the biggest relocation of your life probably. Except for the fact, that you won't have that much storage anymore. But don't throw everything away. Sell or give it away. And if you cannot let go of certain things, if you are emotionally attached, you can still find storage for them, maybe with friends or relatives.
