Van Tour without frills!

Judith February 18, 2020

How to describe our camper build? I guess not a thousand words could describe what a short video tour does. So here you have it: a video van tour of our converted Citroen Jumper. With a high roof, long body and extended wheelbase (L4H3) there is plenty to see.

This build took us about ten months of hard work, tears and a lot of laughter. What is not in the video: the intense planning that went ahead. Please check out our planning process and 3D model. This helped us a lot to visualize and to grasp the whole project.
