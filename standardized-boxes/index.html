Garage Slideout

Dominik February 26, 2020

For the garage slideout, we use heavy duty full extension slides. These slides are meant for heavy loads and unlike the [drawer and fridge slides] they do not have a self retracting or soft closing mechanism but a locking function, that keeps them in place while driving.

DACH: https://amzn.to/388QYI2

USA: https://amzn.to/32vXYgQ

We use standard size boxes (called Euro Boxes) with a side door for easy access when the boxes are stacked. This is both practical and weight saving, as you only need one set of heavy duty slides. We are very happy with this choice, as it keeps the garage organised with a minimal amount of effort.

DACH: https://www.obi.de/aufbewahrungsboxen/obi-eurobox-system-tauro-box-flap-side-40-x-30-x-22-cm-transparent/p/2615755

USA: Unfortunately the Euro Box system is not established in the USA. There seems to be a high demand for these boxes, so if you are interested in trading them in bulk and have experience in importing larger quantities, let us know and we will get you the contact details from the manufacturer.
