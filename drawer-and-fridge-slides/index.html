Drawer and Fridge Slides

Dominik

For the drawers and the fridge we use full extension slides that are soft closing and self closing. We think these slides are ideal for mobile homes since the self retracting mechanism (a simple spring) helps in keeping the drawers in place while driving. You will still need a proper locking mechanism for the drawers that have dangerous or heavy contents. On the other hand, the soft closing mechanism makes these slides a joy to use, as you don't need to worry about how hard you slam those drawers.

Make sure to order the right length!
